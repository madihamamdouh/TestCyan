Copilot Employee Self-Service Agent

Your Expert HR Support Team

  • The Copilot Employee Self-Service Agent is an HR AI-driven virtual assistant integrated within Microsoft 365, designed to help employees quickly access information, submit requests, and resolve common queries such as:

    • How do I apply for leave?

    • What is my vacation balance?

    • Can I get a copy of my payslip?

    • What’s the company’s remote work policy?

    • How do I update my personal information?

  • Our expert team will work closely with your organization to implement this solution effectively, streamlining HR processes while reducing administrative workloads and improve employee satisfaction.
