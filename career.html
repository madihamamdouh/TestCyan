Copilot Specialist

Job Description:

The Copilot Specialist focuses on educating employees and teams on how to effectively use Microsoft 365 Copilot. This role involves designing training programs, conducting workshops, and providing continuous support to help teams integrate Copilot into their daily workflows.

Responsibilities:

  • Design and deliver interactive Microsoft 365 Copilot training programs for businesses.
  • Create guides, tutorials, and knowledge resources for end users.
  • Conduct live training sessions, webinars, and hands-on workshops.
  • Train teams on how to integrate Copilot into daily workflows.
  • Provide one-on-one coaching to help users overcome learning barriers.
  • Gather user feedback to improve training materials and adoption strategies.

Qualifications:

  • Experience in Microsoft 365 adoption.
  • Strong understanding of Microsoft 365 apps and AI-powered productivity tools.
  • Experience in creating and delivering engaging training programs.
  • Ability to communicate complex topics in a simple and engaging manner.
  • Passion for helping teams embrace new technology and improve efficiency.
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills to engage with stakeholders.

Copilot Implementation Consultant

Job Description:

The Microsoft 365 Copilot Consultant is responsible for guiding businesses implement and maximize the benefits of Microsoft Copilot including involves assessing business needs, deploying a tailor Copilot solution, improve productivity, and streamline operations

Responsibilities:

  • Working closely with clients to understand their business needs and design tailored Copilot implementation strategies.
  • Providing expert guidance on how to use Copilot for task automation, document creation, and data insights.
  • Configuring and customizing Copilot to align with business workflows.
  • Collaborating with IT teams to integrate Copilot with existing workflows, data sources, and third-party applications.
  • Ensure data security and compliance within Copilot implementations.
  • Troubleshoot issues and provide ongoing support & optimization.
  • Train end-users on Copilot’s features and best practices.
  • Stay updated on Copilot advancements and recommend new features to clients.

Qualifications:

  • Experience in Microsoft 365 consulting, IT solutions, or AI-powered workplace tools.
  • Strong expertise in Microsoft 365 applications and cloud-based solutions.
  • Knowledge of AI automation and how Copilot enhances business productivity.
  • Problem-solving skills with the ability to diagnose and resolve technical issues.
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills to engage with stakeholders.
